W2t3 Solutions


The end goal for The Registry is the w2t3 solutions - standard business solutions allowing IRL businesses to integrate with The Registry Metaverse. We will providing no-code digital infrastructure (digital space and layout, backend eCommerce workflow etc.) allowing smooth transition from web2 to web3, so that business owners or artists can focus on operating and creating without the need for a web3 developer. Imagine setting up shop in the Registry Metaverse in less than a hour, all by yourself. With the w2t3 solution, The Registry will be a stepping stone towards web3 mass adaption.
